# New Zealand Compliance, Free Access, and Voluntary Support Notes

This planning note does not provide legal or tax advice. MXZTAR Forge must operate
truthfully and consistently with applicable New Zealand law.

## Confirmed access position

- official software use is free of charge;
- there is no timed trial, recurring subscription, or feature paywall in the confirmed
  product model;
- users may voluntarily support the founder at
  `https://buymeacoffee.com/mxztar`;
- donation must not control access to core features or user-owned local files;
- a formal open-source `LICENSE` remains a separate founder decision before release.

## Fair Trading conduct

The product must not mislead users about:

- what the software currently does;
- what AI can observe, infer, or reliably reconstruct;
- whether a feature is implemented, planned, experimental, or unavailable;
- whether a donation is optional;
- what support a donation does or does not purchase;
- what data remains local or crosses a network;
- software licence, warranty, update, compatibility, or support terms;
- GST or tax treatment.

## Donation clarity

Any support message must:

- identify the payment as voluntary support;
- avoid fabricated urgency, scarcity, entitlement, or guilt;
- avoid implying that payment unlocks hidden core functionality;
- link clearly to the external provider before the user leaves Forge;
- describe any actual supporter benefit truthfully if one is introduced later;
- preserve access to local projects regardless of donation status.

## Licence boundary

“Free of charge” describes price. “Open source” also requires explicit legal permission
to use, inspect, modify, and redistribute code under stated conditions. The founder
must select a recognised software licence and contributor policy before the first
public release. Documentation must not invent those permissions in advance.

## GST caution

Do not advertise “+ GST,” promise tax deductibility, or make tax representations unless
business registration, payment-provider configuration, invoicing, and accounting
handling have been confirmed for the actual transaction.
