# NZ Compliance and Subscription Notes

This project must operate according to New Zealand law and fair dealing principles.

## Fair Trading conduct

The product must not mislead users about:

- what the software does
- what the AI can or cannot reliably infer
- free trial duration
- paid start date
- monthly price
- cancellation method
- refund/support policy
- GST/tax treatment

## Subscription clarity

For a 60-day free access model, the user-facing terms must clearly state:

- free access lasts 60 days
- what happens when the free period ends
- whether payment details are collected before the trial
- exact monthly price
- cancellation method
- whether access continues after cancellation until the paid period ends
- support contact
- data/export policy

## GST caution

Do not advertise "+ GST" unless the business is correctly GST-registered and checkout/accounting handling is ready.
